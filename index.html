EOR Platform Compliance Incident Database
Subscription

HR and legal teams evaluating Deel, Remote, or Rippling have no way to look up that platform's actual compliance failures — misclassifications, market exits, tax penalties, data breaches. The platforms never disclose it. The data is buried in labor board filings and court records.

🌊
Why blue ocean
No neutral incident database exists. Review sites (G2, Capterra) don't cover compliance failures. SERP for "Deel Brazil misclassification" is empty — high-intent procurement queries with zero competition.
💰
Monetization
HR/legal team subscription $99–299/mo. Compliance law firms pay for ad placement targeting procurement searches. High-ticket B2B with clear budget.
🚀
Cold start
"[Platform] [country] compliance failure 2024" — one page per platform per country, zero competition. EOR procurement communities on LinkedIn and Slack are immediate distribution.
Solar Interconnection Rules by Utility
Subscription

Residential solar installers operate across 3,000+ US utilities, each with its own interconnection process, capacity limits, net metering status, approval timeline, and paperwork requirements. There's no aggregated database — installers research each utility from scratch every time.

🌊
Why blue ocean
3,000 utilities × their individual PDFs = an impossible research task at scale. No aggregator has built this. The fragmentation is the moat — once you have the data, no one can replicate it quickly.
💰
Monetization
Installer subscription + per-seat pricing for larger firms. Solar equipment financiers and lenders also need this data — B2B lead-gen upside.
🚀
Cold start
"[Utility name] solar interconnection process" — one programmatic page per utility, none ranked yet. Installer Facebook groups and NABCEP forums are day-one distribution.
Used Baby Gear Recall & Safety Check
Affiliate

Parents buying second-hand car seats, cribs, and strollers have no quick way to verify whether a specific model has been recalled, expired, or no longer meets current federal safety standards. CPSC search is clunky. Resale platforms don't warn you. The consequence of getting it wrong is real.

🌊
Why blue ocean
No tool gives a model-specific safety verdict in one lookup. SERP for "[model name] recall" is mostly news articles and PDFs — not a clean tool. High emotional stakes drive search volume.
💰
Monetization
Free check → "unsafe, here's a compliant replacement" affiliate link. Resale shops pay for batch verification subscriptions. High conversion because the unsafe verdict creates immediate purchase intent.
🚀
Cold start
"[Model name] recalled" queries — one page per product model, hundreds of models, each with buying intent. Facebook parenting groups and Buy Nothing communities are zero-cost distribution.
Alpha-Gal Syndrome Ingredient Checker
Subscription

Alpha-gal syndrome patients — triggered by a lone star tick bite — must avoid all mammalian animal products including hidden ingredients in medications, supplements, and vaccines. Gelatin, stearic acid, and lactose appear in pills with no warning. Patients research every product by hand.

🌊
Why blue ocean
Diagnosed cases are growing yearly. No tool covers the medication and supplement side — only food. Facebook support groups have thousands of members asking "is this pill safe?" daily, with no tool to answer.
💰
Monetization
Ad-free subscriber tier + affiliate to alpha-gal-safe brands. Patient communities pay for peace of mind — the use case is medical-grade necessity, not casual curiosity.
🚀
Cold start
"Is [medication/supplement] alpha-gal safe" — programmatic long-tail SEO on every common drug + supplement. The Alpha-Gal Association and patient Facebook groups are a warm audience of tens of thousands.
Homelab Hardware & Firmware Checker
Subscription

Self-hosters buy cheap enterprise hardware on eBay only to find the firmware is locked, the license is iCloud-bound, security updates stopped two years ago, or the fan noise makes it unsuitable for home use. Reddit threads are the only source of truth — scattered and outdated.

🌊
Why blue ocean
No tool aggregates firmware availability, license transferability, OS compatibility (Proxmox/TrueNAS/OpenWrt), noise levels, and idle wattage per model. This data lives in fragmented forum threads.
💰
Monetization
Subscription for model alerts + used hardware dealer affiliate referrals. Hobbyists in this space spend serious money — average homelab setup runs $500–$3,000.
🚀
Cold start
r/homelab and Hacker News are the immediate distribution channel. "[Model] Proxmox compatible / [model] fan noise homelab" long-tail SEO captures pre-purchase research with no competing tool.
Dog Food Nutrient Database for Medical Diets
Affiliate

Dogs diagnosed with kidney disease, bladder stones, or copper storage disease need a low-phosphorus or low-purine diet. Vets prescribe expensive prescription brands. Owners want to know: does a cheaper non-prescription food actually meet the same nutritional threshold? No one publishes this data.

🌊
Why blue ocean
Prescription pet food is a $2B+ market kept opaque by vets and manufacturers. No neutral tool maps brand × condition × actual nutrient levels. SERP for "kidney disease dog food phosphorus comparison" is dominated by prescription brand sites.
💰
Monetization
Affiliate on recommended non-prescription alternatives + subscription for full database access. Pet health is one of the highest-converting affiliate verticals — owners spend without hesitation when a vet says the dog needs it.
🚀
Cold start
"[Brand] dog food phosphorus content / kidney safe" — programmatic page per SKU × condition. Dog health subreddits and Facebook breed health groups have owners actively searching for exactly this.

The Pipeline

5 filters. Most niches don't survive.

Any niche that passes all five is added to the weekly drop. Most don't.

🔍

1 · Pain validation

Does this problem recur in forums, app reviews, and search? Is there genuine frustration, not just mild inconvenience?

📊

2 · SEO gap analysis

Is the SERP actually empty? Are there rankable long-tail variations no one has claimed? One dominant player kills the opportunity.

💰

3 · Monetization clarity

Is there a clear path to revenue — affiliate, subscription, lead-gen, or SaaS? "Maybe ads" doesn't count.

🛠️

4 · Build feasibility

Can one person build a useful v1 in a weekend? Does it require proprietary data no indie hacker can access?

📣

5 · Reachable audience

Is there a community, subreddit, forum, or niche platform where the first 100 users already gather?
