Privacy Policy

Last updated: 11 June 2026

Gauntrix is operated by Friday Lab (Australia). This policy explains what data we collect, why, and how we handle it.

What we collect

Email address — when you join the waitlist or subscribe, we collect your email address. We use it only to send you the weekly niche opportunity drops and product updates you signed up for.

Analytics — we use GoatCounter for page view analytics. GoatCounter does not use cookies, does not track individuals, and does not share data with third parties. It records only aggregate counts (pages visited, referrer, country).

What we don't collect

We do not collect names, phone numbers, payment card details, or any sensitive personal information on this site. Payment processing is handled entirely by Lemon Squeezy — we never see your card details.

Third-party services

FormSubmit.co — processes waitlist form submissions and stores email addresses on our behalf. See their privacy policy.

Lemon Squeezy — handles subscription payments. See their privacy policy.

GitHub Pages — hosts this website. GitHub may log IP addresses as part of standard server operation.

How we use your email

We use your email address to send the weekly Gauntrix drops you subscribed to, and occasional product updates. We do not sell, rent, or share your email address with any third party for marketing purposes.

Unsubscribe

Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link. You can opt out at any time with one click. We will remove your address promptly.

Data retention

We retain your email address for as long as you remain subscribed. If you unsubscribe, your address is deleted from our mailing list within 30 days.

Your rights (Australian Privacy Act 1988)

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, email us at tony@fridaylab.io and we will respond within 30 days.
